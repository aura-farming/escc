# Jurisdiction — United States (CAN-SPAM)

Applies when the recipient is in the US (`jurisdiction-routing.md`). CAN-SPAM is opt-out (not opt-in) for commercial email, but the floor is still mandatory.

## Headers & subject
- Accurate "from", "to", and routing information; the originating identity must be truthful.
- No deceptive subject lines — the subject must reflect the content.

## Identification
- Identify the message as an advertisement where applicable, and include a valid **physical postal address**.

## Opt-out
- Provide a clear, working opt-out and honor it within **10 business days**. Do not charge a fee, require more than a single step, or demand more than an opt-out preference.
- After opt-out, do not sell or transfer the address.

## Notes
- Each separate violation is subject to penalties; liability can attach to the company whose product is promoted, not just the sender.
