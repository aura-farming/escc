# Jurisdiction — Australia (Spam Act 2003)

Applies when the recipient is in Australia (`jurisdiction-routing.md`).

## Consent
- A commercial electronic message requires **consent** — express (opt-in) or **inferred** (an existing business relationship, or a conspicuously published work address relevant to the role, construed narrowly).
- Keep evidence of the basis (`lawful-basis.md`).

## Sender identity
- Accurately identify the sender (the individual/organisation that authorised the message) and include contact details that stay valid for at least 30 days.

## Unsubscribe
- Include a functional, low-cost unsubscribe facility. Honor opt-outs within **5 business days**; the facility must work for at least 30 days after the message.

## Notes
- Applies to email, SMS, and instant messaging. Penalties are per-message and significant — when consent is uncertain, do not send.
