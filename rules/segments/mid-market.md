# Segment — Mid-Market

The balance segment: real evaluation rigor, but faster and leaner than enterprise.

## Cycle & stages
- Moderate cycle (weeks to a quarter). Lighter procurement than enterprise but still a real decision process.
- A POC/evaluation is common but time-boxed (`evaluation-plan`).

## Stakeholder depth
- Smaller buying committee (often 2–4 roles). Multi-thread to the economic buyer plus a champion; do not over-engineer the committee map.

## Cadence overrides
- Balanced volume and personalization. Tighter cadences than enterprise; still researched, never blast.
- Mixed activity targets: meetings and pipeline creation both matter (`targets.md`).
