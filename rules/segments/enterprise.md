# Segment — Enterprise

Large, multi-stakeholder accounts with long cycles. Overrides the common defaults where enterprise reality differs.

## Cycle & stages
- Long cycle (commonly two-plus quarters). The cycle-length floor is high; a "commit" inside a few weeks with early MEDDPICC is a red flag (`meddpicc/forecast-risk`).
- Mandatory security review and procurement/legal paper process (`paper-process`); start them early.

## Stakeholder depth
- Assume a buying committee of many roles; **multi-thread by default** ("multi-thread-before-close" seed). Single-threaded enterprise deals are high-risk.
- Champion development and an economic-buyer relationship are non-negotiable (`stakeholder-mapping`).

## Cadence overrides
- Lower volume, higher personalization and research per touch. Account-based, not blast; longer, more spaced cadences; executive tone.
- Activity targets favor meetings and relationships over raw dial volume (`targets.md`).
