# Segment — SMB

Small businesses: short cycles, few stakeholders, velocity matters.

## Cycle & stages
- Short cycle (days to weeks). Often a single decision-maker who is also the economic buyer.
- Minimal paper process; speed-to-lead and momentum win ("speed-to-lead-within-SLA" seed).

## Stakeholder depth
- Usually single-threaded by nature; confirm the one decision-maker IS the economic buyer rather than assuming a committee.

## Cadence overrides
- Higher volume, lighter (but still relevant) personalization; concise messaging; one clear CTA.
- Activity targets favor volume leading indicators (dials, conversations, meetings) within compliance (`targets.md`, `outbound-compliance`).
