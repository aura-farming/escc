# MEDDPICC — Qualification

Qualify with evidence, not optimism. Each letter is a field to fill from sourced facts (`selling-principles`), captured in HubSpot via `crm-operator`, and probed in `call-prep` / `discovery-notes`.

## The model
- **M — Metrics:** the quantified business value/impact the buyer needs. A number, not a vibe.
- **E — Economic buyer:** the person with discretionary budget authority. Named, met, and engaged.
- **D — Decision criteria:** the buyer's explicit criteria for choosing.
- **D — Decision process:** the steps, dates, and approvals to a signature.
- **P — Paper process:** legal/procurement path — MSA/DPA/order form (`paper-process`).
- **I — Identify pain:** the compelling reason to act now; without it, no deal.
- **C — Champion:** an internal advocate with influence who sells when you are not in the room.
- **C — Competition:** the alternatives, including "do nothing" and the incumbent.

## Discipline
- A field is "known" only with evidence; otherwise it is a **gap** to probe next, not an assumption.
- Champion vs. coach: a coach gives information; a champion has power and uses it. Test the champion.
- Qualification is continuous — re-test as the deal moves stages (`rules/lifecycle-stages.md`).
