# ESCC v1.8.0 — The source-of-truth release

_Released 2026-07-07 · [CHANGELOG](../../CHANGELOG.md) · [ADR-0018](../DECISIONS.md)_

The v1.7.1 strategic review found three structural facts keeping ESCC at
~40% of a source of truth: account identity was lossy (one company fragmented
into disjoint stores), the learning loop was fully built but **starved** (an
empty outcomes ledger, a correction signal with no writer), and "HubSpot
wins" was prose. v1.8.0 fixes all three — plus the query, audit, autonomy,
and currency surfaces that make the truth usable.

## Highlights

- **One identity per account.** "Example Co", "example-co.example", "jane@example-co.example", and
  `company:<hubspot-id>` now name ONE store across account-memory, voice, promises,
  and governance. Link once (`escc identity link`), heal history reversibly
  (`escc identity backfill`), and erasure reaches every fragment
  (`privacy-purge` expands the whole cluster).
- **The harness finally compounds.** Deal-stage writes and booked meetings
  auto-land in the outcomes ledger; replies are one attest command; explicit
  rep corrections become pending instincts behind the human gate. The
  confidence-weighting machinery that ran empty since v1.0 now has fuel.
- **"HubSpot wins" is enforced.** `escc reconcile` diffs memory against a
  live CRM snapshot and syncs it with provenance — deal-status loops
  auto-close on closed-won/lost; human promises are never touched.
- **Ask for the truth.** `escc truth <account>` (and the `/truth` skill)
  answers "what do we actually know" with every section labeled by source and
  last-verified time — and says plainly when a live CRM read is missing.
- **Prove compliance.** `escc audit --recipient jane@example-co.example` answers "show
  every outbound decision for this person" from the governance ledger.
- **Wrong numbers refused.** Mixed-currency roll-ups no longer silently sum
  AUD + USD: amounts normalize through a workspace FX table (rate + as-of
  provenance) or land in a `skipped` list — never in the total.

## The guarantee (why this is safe)

The fail-closed send-gate changed in exactly two ways, **both tightening**:
one governance read instead of three (same verdicts — the whole existing gate
suite passes unchanged), and the strict-profile separation-of-duties branch
(tests prove the standard profile and clean four-gates tokens are
byte-identical to v1.7.1, and that a rep-signed override token stops passing
the moment a workspace tightens to strict). The self-digest token is bound to
the exact recipient + content — tests prove it cannot launder a prospect
draft. Outcome payloads are whitelisted structured fields; corrections skip
long prompts (likely pasted third-party content); everything learned still
passes the I7 human review gate. `crm-operator` remains the sole CRM writer —
reconcile and backfill only ever write locally.

## Upgrade notes

- **Run the migration once:** `escc identity backfill` (dry-run), review the
  plan, then `--apply`. Every touched file is backed up first; restore =
  copy the backups back. Then link your key accounts:
  `escc identity link "Example Co Pty Ltd" company:<hubspot-id>`.
- **Multi-currency teams:** copy `config/locale.example.json` to
  `<data-home>/escc/config/locale.json` and set your reporting currency + FX
  rates. Without it, cross-currency totals refuse (by design).
- **Scheduled sweeps (optional):** `escc watch --install-schedule` (macOS)
  then run the printed `launchctl load` command.
- Catalog: **68 skills, 18 agents, 70 commands, 30 hook matchers**. New env:
  `ESCC_LOOP_STALE_DAYS`, `ESCC_OVERRIDE_REQUIRES_MANAGER`.
- Bare all-digit account ids are now read as HubSpot company ids (legacy
  bare-digit stems merge at backfill — documented in ADR-0018).

## Scope & deferred (per the roadmap)

Team-shared truth layer, play-runner, win-loss→battlecard candidates,
warehouse reads, e-signature: v1.9.0/v2.0.0 (each carries an ADR reversal or
a net-new store). Resonance-suggest stays off until the ledger accrues
history — outcome weighting compounds over weeks by design (OUTCOME_STEP
0.05, capped).
